Instructions

  • Students must present valid photo identification before starting the exam.
  • Students are not allowed to use any external devices such as smartphones, calculators, or notes during the exam.
  • Leaving fullscreen mode or opening additional tabs or windows during the exam will result in immediate termination of the exam and may lead to disqualification or other disciplinary actions.
  • Students are not allowed to communicate with anyone else during the exam, either verbally or through electronic means.
  • Students must manage their time effectively as per the exam duration specified.
  • Any form of cheating, including looking at unauthorized materials, copying from others, or using online resources, is strictly prohibited.
  • The exam will be monitored by invigilators either physically or through video surveillance to ensure compliance with the rules.
  • Students must submit their answers within the specified time limit. Late submissions may not be accepted.
  • Violations of these rules may result in disqualification from the exam or other disciplinary actions as per the institution's policies.
